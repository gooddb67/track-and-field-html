Meet the Cast

Casey Sinclair (Petey) has been acting on stage as a member of the Altadena Playhouse since he was three years old. His favorite sport to play is baseball, and his favorite sport to watch is basketball.

Elle Powers (Julia) is a certified triple threat! She’s a soloist with her local church group, was named first runner up on the competition series “You Betcha Christian Kids Can Dance” and now, she’s a leading young lady.

J.T. Francis (Fudgeman) loves food in real life too! He likes making macaroni and pizza and hanging out with his friends.

Buzzy Goldwin (Coach P) is an experienced screen actor with featured roles in the pilots “1 Dad 1 Mom” and “Noah’s Arkansas”.

Steven (as himself) is an actor, philanthropist, entrepreneur, healer, athlete, physicist and more. Star of countless films for Alaco - of which he is President and CEO - Steven has created a new style of acting entitled radical feeling, in which through the power of touch, he can experience any emotion. Steven teaches this acting style exclusively at Alaco headquarters and its Summer Camp in The Mohave Desert. Steven lives in Los Angeles with his dog Bonezy, his son Tate, and his 400 wives.
